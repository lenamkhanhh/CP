    • Each vertex i is embedded as a point (xi , yi , 0) in the space. The coordinates xi and yi must be integers between
      0 and 400, inclusive. All points must have distinct coordinates.

A simple cycle of a graph is a sequence of three or more distinct vertices (v1 , v2 , . . . , vc ) such that there exists an edge
connecting vertices vi and vi+1 for each 1 ≤ i < c and there also exists an edge connecting vertices v1 and vc . For
